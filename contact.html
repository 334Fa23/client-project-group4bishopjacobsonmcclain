Customer Information

Payment Process

Step One. Searching..

Teams may browse or ask about any packages that we offer.

Step Two. Ordering..

Teams order their preferred event travel package and sign a contract.

Step Three. First Payment..

The customers pay their initial deposit within 30 days of the signed contract.

Step Four. Follow up..

TitleTown Events will reach out roughly 90+ days out to ask about number of rooms your team anticipates and the number of estimated travelers in your party.

Step Five. Second Payment..

After all information is gathered in step 4, we will invoice the remaining amount. Full payments must be payed 60 days prior to the event.

Step Six. Trip Starts..

The day is finally here! Your team departs and travels to Hawaii. Your time in paradise is just beginning
