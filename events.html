Events

The 2024 D2 Big Island Classic

The 2024 D2 Big Island Classic, held in November and December, will feature your team vs University of Hawaii at Hilo and other participating D2 teams. The November tournament travel dates are scheduled for November 25th-December 1st with games being played November 29th-30th. The December tournament will take place the 20th-22nd and the travel dates are schduled for the 17th-23rd.

All games will be played in Afook-Chinen Civic Auditorium in Hilo, Hawaii.

1st Annual Ulili Softball Invitational

During 2024, TitleTown Events is hosting the Ulili Softball Invitational within the month of February. The 2-event invitational takes place February 8th-10th and February 15th-17th. The tournament includes 5 games, pre-game equipment, game personnel, and an awards ceremony!

All games will be played at the Mauliola Sport Complex.

O'AHU Spring Break Soccer Trip

Both of the Spring Break Soccer Invitationals are hosted in Honolulu, Hawaii. There is currently no set date for either soccer invitational as of yet. As we are in the process of selecting a date around the Spring Break of Hawaii's universities, teams have been selected and set for each invitational. One invitational includes Hawaii Pacific University, Chaminade University, and the Hawaii 18u Men's Club Team. The other invitational includes the University of Hawaii, Hawaii Pacific University, and Chaminade University.

All games will be played at the Mauliola Sport Complex.
