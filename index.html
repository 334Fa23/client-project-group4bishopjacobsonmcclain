Welcome to TitleTown Events LLC, your hub for collegiate sporting events!

Our mission is simple: we're here to host exciting collegiate tournaments for NAIA and NCAA affiliated universities and colleges.

What drives us? Ensuring that we meet the unique needs of every team, every coach, and every athlete who joins our events.

Our values guide us every step of the way:

  • Integrity: Doing what's right, always.
  • Commitment: We're dedicated to the best interest of officials, teams, coaches, and athletes.
  • Service: Creating unforgettable experiences for players and coaches.
  • Growth: Expanding programs to benefit the collegiate sporting community.

At TitleTown Events LLC, we're not just about helping you win on the field, but we're also about winning the hearts and minds of every team with our integrity, unbeatable service, and continuous dedication to the collegiate sports community. Let TitleTown Events LLC work for you - where sports come to life!

Project Timeline

01 Event Setup

Once we confirm with the Hawai'i universities and tournament directors about events, our team at TitleTown Events start to create flyers and advertisements to get colleges interested in making these events happen!

02 Initial Contact

TitleTown Events begins their working relationship with you, getting to know your team, your needs, and your expectations while on the island.

03 Travel Package

Once you've reviewed the information from the intial contract and have confirmed your team will participate in this event, it's time to choose one of three travel packages that TitleTown Events provides.

04 Contract Signed

Once our team receives confirmation on the travel package you've selected, TitleTown Events will then create a contract for your team. An initial deposit is due 30 days after the contract is signed.

05 Land in Hawai'i

Transportation will pick you up at the airport and will take you to your designated hotem. From there, your experience is just beginning! Your games, practices, and extra activities will be awaiting you in the upcoming days.

06 Travel Home

Unfortunately, your time in paradise has come to an end. Our team at TitleTown Events looks forward to seeing you come back in the future!
