What We Do

Title town events is a sporting travel agency based out of Hawaii. We specialize in basketball, softball, and soccer, bringing teams out of 44 of the 50 states out to Hawaii to play competitively for the NCAA and NAIA sporting leagues. We offer ground transportation included in your selected package once you land in Hawaii, along with lodging, and extra activities outside of the sporting events.

Packages and pricing

We have three standard packages to help fit your teams budget. For our spring break season, we have the basic package, costing $1000 per person, which includes all ground transportation during your time here, lodging at the courtyard by Marriott Waikiki beach for 6 nights and 7 days, and activities such as The Pearl Harbor Arizona Memorial Tour, and the Diamond Head of Makau’s Lighthouse Hike The Standard Package, costing $1,100 per person, includes all ground transportation during your time here, lodging at courtyard by Marriott Waikiki beach for 6 nights and 7 days, and activities such as the Pearl Harbor Arizona Memorial Tour, and the Traditional Hawaiian Luau. The premium package, costing $1,200 per person, includes ground transportation during your time here, lodging at the courtyard by Marriott Waikiki beach for 6 nights and 7 days, and activities such as the Pearl harbor Arizona memorial tour, diamond head or makeup’s lighthouse hike, traditional Hawaiian luau, and leis upon hotel arrival
