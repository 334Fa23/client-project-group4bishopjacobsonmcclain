What We Do

Title town events is a sporting travel agency based out of Hawaii. We specialize in basketball, softball, and soccer, bringing teams from 44 of the 50 states out to Hawaii to play competitively for the NCAA and NAIA sporting leagues. We offer ground transportation included in your selected package once you land in Hawaii, along with lodging, and extra activities outside of the sporting events.We have three standard packages for each invotation to help fit your teams budget.
