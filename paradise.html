Paradise Cove Luau

The Paradise Cove Luau is an unforgettable experience located in Oahu, Hawai'i. As guests arrive, they can participate in traditional Hawaiian activities, such as flower lei-making or hula dancing, The evening then continues with a stunning sunset ceremony, followed by a delicious buffet dinner featuring traditional Hawaiian cuisine. The night concludes with an exciting Polynesian cultural show, featuring stunning fire knife dancers and live music and dance performances. The Paradise Cove Luau is a must-see attraction for anyone visiting Hawai'i and is a perfect way to immerse yourself in the rich culture and traditions of the islands.
