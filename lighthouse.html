Makapu'u Lighthouse Trail

The Makapu'u Lighthouse Trail is a picturesque hike located on the southeastern tip of Oahu in Hawaii. The trail is approximately two miles round-trip and offers stunning views of the ocean and surrounding islands. The highlight of the trail is the Makapu'u Lighthouse, which stands on a scenic cliff 600 feet above sea level and has been guiding ships since 1909.

The trail is easy to moderate and suitable for all skill levels, making it a popular destination for families. Additionally, hikers may spot whales and seabirds along the way.
