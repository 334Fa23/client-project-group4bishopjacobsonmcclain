Frequently Asked Questions

Parent Travel

What is included in the parent activity package?

Each parent and or member who purchases this package will receive the exact same activity package as the team (Basic, Standard, or Premium). Our activities include the Arizona Memorial Tour at Pearl Harbor National Park. a choice between Diamond Head or the Makapu'u Lighthouse Trail, and a traditional Hawaiian Luau at Paradise Cove.

What if I want to schedule another activity while on the Island?

That will be up to you and your family. We can help with getting you the contact information for the other arrangements but it will not be included in the parent activity package and will not be booked through us.

What happens if an activity is canceled due to weather?

If an activity is canceled we will do our best to reschedule during the week of the trip. If we cannot accommodate that adjustment then there is no refund for the activity missed.

Is transportation with the team provided?

Unfortunately, transportation is not provided for the activities. You must align your own transportation through Maryville Travel, carshare companies (Uber, Lyft, Turo, etc.), or go through a rental car company at the airport.

What is the duration of these activities?

The Arizona Tour plus the Pearl Harbor National Park tour can take anywhere from one-three hours. Both trails take about thirty to forty minutes and the Luau lasts from 5:00 pm to 9:00 pm.
